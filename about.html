Our Story

Welcome to Red Lantern, a neighborhood spot inspired by classic Chinese flavors and the warmth of family-style dining. From day one, our focus has been simple: fresh ingredients, time-honored techniques, and generous hospitality.

Our signature dishes — from smoky Kung Pao Chicken to comforting Wonton Soup and hearty Chow Mein — are cooked to order in hot woks for deep, satisfying flavor. Whether you’re here for a casual lunch or a lively dinner with friends, we’re thrilled to serve you.

Thank you for being part of our story. We can’t wait to cook for you again.
